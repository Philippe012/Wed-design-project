PARTENERS AND COLLABORATORS

Agritech Harvest Hub plays a pivotal role in revolutionizing agricultural practices in Rwanda through strategic collaborations and partnerships with key institutions. The Ministry of Agriculture and Animal Resources (MINAGRI) stands as a foundational partner, providing a regulatory framework and crucial insights that align Agritech Harvest Hub with national agricultural policies. This partnership ensures that the hub operates within established guidelines, fostering sustainability and compliance. Collaborating with the National Agricultural Export Development Board (NAEB) enhances Agritech Harvest Hub's reach, facilitating the export of agricultural products. The partnership with the University of Rwanda signifies a commitment to research and development, allowing the integration of cutting-edge technologies and innovative solutions into agricultural processes.

Agritech Harvest Hub has forged alliances with transport companies to streamline the distribution process, ensuring fresh produce reaches its destination efficiently. Concurrently, partnerships with financial institutions offer farmers accessible funding and resources, empowering them to adopt advanced farming technique. By weaving a network of collaborators, Agritech Harvest Hub aspires to create a comprehensive ecosystem that not only supports farmers but also contributes to the economic growth of Rwanda. These partnerships underscore Agritech Harvest Hub's dedication to leveraging collective expertise and resources for the betterment of the agricultural community, ultimately fostering sustainable development.



In a transformative alliance, Transport Company in Rwanda partners with
AgriTech Harvest Hub to revolutionize the agricultural supply chain. By leveraging
RITCO's efficient logistics and transportation services, the collaboration ensures
seamless delivery of harvested produce, reducing post-harvest losses and contributing
to the overall success of agricultural initiatives, strengthening the agricultural
sector in Rwanda.


National Agricultural Export Development Board, abbreviated as “NAEB” is a commercial public
institution established by the law No 13/2017 of 14/04/2017 determining its mission, organisation
and functioning. It has legal personality and enjoys administrative and financial autonomy and is
also managed in accordance with relevant laws.



The Ministry of Agriculture and Animal Resources (MINAGRI) has the mission of promoting
the sustainable development of a modern, efficient and competitive agriculture and livestock
sector, in order to ensure food security, agriculture export and diversification of the productions
for the benefit of the farmer and the economy of the Country.




OUR VISION Bank of Kigali, in collaboration with AgriTech Harvest Hub, is fostering agricultural development in Rwanda.
This strategic partnership aims to provide financial support and innovative solutions, empowering farmers and
enhancing the efficiency of agricultural practices, ultimately contributing to the country's economic growth
and food security.


The University of Rwanda's collaboration with AgriTech Harvest Hub signifies a powerful alliance driving agricultural
innovation and education. By merging academic expertise with technological advancements, this partnership cultivates
a sustainable farming ecosystem. Together, they aim to equip students and farmers with cutting-edge knowledge, fostering
a dynamic agricultural landscape that addresses the challenges of the future.



Agritech Harvest Hub's collaboration with financial institutions bridges the gap between traditional and digital realms, fostering
agricultural growth. Through conventional financial partnerships, farmers gain access to funds for equipment and resources. Simultaneously,
the hub explores digital solutions, ensuring seamless transactions and financial inclusivity. This dual approach not only
empowers farmers financially but also propels the agricultural sector into the digital age, enhancing efficiency and sustainability
in AgriTech Harvest Hub's mission.
